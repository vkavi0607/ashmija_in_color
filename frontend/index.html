Our Story

About ashmija in color

Founded in 2015 in Chennai, ashmija in color began with a simple belief — every wall is a canvas waiting to tell a story. What started as a passion project by a small group of mural enthusiasts has grown into South Asia's most trusted mural art studio.

We've painted over 750,000 sq. ft. across 38+ cities, partnering with brands like Google, WeWork, Taj Hotels, and ITC to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences. Our curated network of 120+ artists specialises in everything from botanical murals to abstract installations.

Passion-Driven

Every project is treated as a work of art, not just a job.

Community First

We empower local artists and invest in emerging talent.

Eco-Conscious

100% eco-certified, UV-resistant paints on every project.

Our Curation & Network

At ashmija in color, we maintain a highly curated network of over 120 professional muralists, traditional painters, graffiti artists, and sculptors across South Asia.

Our artists are selected not only for their exceptional visual craftsmanship but also for their understanding of architectural scale, safety standards, and durable material applications. We handle the entire lifecycle: design licensing, digital mockup staging, scaffold logistics, and weather-proof varnishing.
